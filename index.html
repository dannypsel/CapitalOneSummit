Longest dispatch time

Which areas take the longest time to dispatch to on average? How can this be reduced?

  • Visitacion Valley and Portola

    Zipcode: 94134

    I implemented a function that analyzes the data and compares the dispatch time and response time and found the area with the largest difference. The area that had the largest dispatch time was Visitacion Valley and Portola. We can reduce the dispatch time by giving higher priority to regions that have a long dispatch time. Additionally, the majority of the dispatch calls are for medical reasons, hence medical personnels should familiarize themselves with traffic patterns and local road systems.

Preparing for the Future

Which areas are experiencing the greatest increase in dispatch calls?
Where and what type of dispatch service would you place to help with the rate of increasing calls?

  • TwinPeaks-Glen Park and Western Addition/Japantown

    My algorithm examined the data and compared the first three days of dispatch calls with all 12 dadys of dispatch calls for each zip code and saved the difference. It then returns the two areas with the largest inrease in dispatch calls. The results I got were Twinpeaks-Glen Park and Western Addition/Japantown. The type of dispatch service most needed in those two areas were both medical incident, possibly indicating that moer ambulances and medical personnel are needed in these two areas.
